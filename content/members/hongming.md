__Biography__

Hongming did her bachelor in Bioengineering at Yangtze University in Jinzhou, Hubei. She then joined Nongyue He’s lab at the Hunan University of Technology and the Southeast University as a Master student, studying the multiplexed nucleic acid detection of diarrheagenic Escherichia coli under Song Li. After that, she went to join Naibo Yang’s group at the Beijing Genomics Institution-Shenzhen and the University of Chinese Academy of Science as a PhD student, studying the genome of shark IgNAR loci. In 2022, she joined the BeiGene as a research investigator, working on techniques of antibody discovery for cancer treatment. In 2024, she joined Xi Chen’s lab at the Southern University of Science and Technology as a Postdoc, studying the epigenetic changes during early embryogenesis.
